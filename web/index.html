Our Philosophy

Recovery happens in community — not in isolation.

Peace of Life Homes is not a treatment center. We are recovery residences — real homes in real neighborhoods — where individuals in recovery find the structure, accountability, and peer support that make lasting sobriety possible.

Every home is alcohol and drug-free. Every resident shares in the responsibility of maintaining a clean, respectful, and supportive household. We believe that dignity, stability, and connection are the foundation of recovery.

Our Homes

Designed for dignity. Built for recovery.

Private & Shared Accommodations

Well-appointed rooms in single-family homes throughout Richmond. Every residence is fully furnished, with shared living spaces, modern kitchens, and outdoor areas for reflection and community.

Safety Without Compromise

Keypad entry with individual access codes, exterior surveillance, motion-sensor lighting, and enforced curfew. Residents and families can trust that security is never an afterthought.

Structure That Supports Recovery

Weekly house meetings led by an on-site house manager, shared chores and responsibilities, clear expectations, and a zero-tolerance policy for alcohol and drug use create accountability that sticks.

Who We Serve

A home for every step of the journey.

Transitioning from Treatment

For individuals stepping down from inpatient or clinical programs, our residences provide the bridge between intensive care and independent living — with the structure to make the transition successful.

Building a Foundation

For those already on their recovery path who need a stable, substance-free environment to continue growing. Peer support, accountability, and community make the difference.

Seeking a Clean Living Environment

No formal program enrollment required. If you simply want to live in a safe, structured, alcohol and drug-free home, you are welcome here.
